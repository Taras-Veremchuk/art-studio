About me

I'm Nadia from Ukraine. I am 44 years old. I'm a mother of three wonderful sons. I'm a Christian and an artist.

My calling is to be a light to the world. Besides that, God has gifted me with the talent to reflect a fragment of the beauty, grandeur, and unmatched creation in His canvas.

Unforgettable, vibrant childhood and youth were spent first in art school and later in the Uzhhorod College of Arts and Crafts. In the foothills of the picturesque Carpathian Mountains. In a creative atmosphere, among talented professional artists. After college, I worked as a master of production training and a teacher of drawing, painting, and composition. Later, I worked as a portrait artist at the G_Art_Ukraine studio for Paint your life.

Happiness and satisfaction come from bringing joy to others. Because every person is wonderfully beautiful and unique! The image, the gaze, the smile..

The process of creating paintings

The first step involves outlining the face and features with light and dark lines, creating a foundation for my work. Breaking the face into blocks helps transition from general shapes to details, akin to constructing layers of vitality. I experiment with gradients for smooth transitions from light to dark, emphasizing shadows for volume and form. My color palette, like a fairy tale, comprises a limited range reflecting the mood and emotion I aim to convey. Adding details is my favorite part, treating each hair strand and contour like a puzzle piece for a lively image. Once all details are in place, the final touch conveys both outward appearance and inner emotions through facial expressions
